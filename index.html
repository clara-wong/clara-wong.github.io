Hi, I'm Clara Wong. Currently in my third year at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill (UNC-CH), I'm majoring in business administration (specializing in marketing and finance) with a double major in advertising. Altogether, it's kind of a mouthful.
I'm a current development intern at NC Area Health Education Center (AHEC), where I do everything from designing print and online deliverables, to conducting market research, to untangling the mystery of Excel.
I believe in the magic of film photography and trendspotting. I travel by book, and am fueled by ramen and coffee (but not together).
