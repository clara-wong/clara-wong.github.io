Hi, my name is Clara Wong. I am a third-year at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill (UNC-CH) majoring in business administration (specializing in marketing) with a double major in advertising. I'm a current development intern at NC Area Health Education Center (AHEC), where I do everything from designing print and online deliverables, to conducting market research, to untangling the mystery of Excel.
Outside the classroom, I am a freelance photographer and associate beauty editor. I believe in the magic of photography and trendspotting. I travel by book, and am fueled by ramen and coffee (but not together).
